ALESSANDRO CELLI is a freelance research-led industrial designer based between The Hague (NL) and Milan (IT). Recent work has addressed the physicality of data infrastructures and the landscape they create, as well as the inconsistencies of computational predictions.
Trained in product design at Politecnico di Milano (IT) and in design at the Royal Academy of Arts (NL), he is now interested in the history and theory of new technologies and their impact on the environment & society.
Design aside he is involved in music production and sound design.
