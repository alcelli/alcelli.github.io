Alessandro Celli
About

Alessandro Celli is a research – led industrial designer based between The Hague (NL) and Milan (IT).

Recent work has addressed the physicality of data infrastructures and the landscape they create, as well as computational predictions' surreal mechanisms.

Design aside he is interested in music production and sound design (soon to be released).
